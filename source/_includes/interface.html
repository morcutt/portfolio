Platform Redesign / Hackathon

Developed over a hackathon weekend, this project explored a new platform artchitecture that revolved around the user's profile and identity within the community. A story and prototype was created to visualize and communicate new feature development that attempts to guide user experience by hinting at next steps in using the software based interests, connections, and past interaction behavior.

Interactive Email

In an effort to avert login bariers to participation, interactive email was implimented for all member, group, and discussion messaging. Users are now able to respond to colleges and prticipate in discussions from their inbox without having to log into the platform.

Overview

All new features or architecture changes follow a user centerd methodology of researching design patterns and testing solutions to increase participation.

Work is done close with product management, engineering, testing and sales teams from the design process and prototyping through to the feature buildout and release.
