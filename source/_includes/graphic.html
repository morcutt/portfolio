Within3 Collaboration Community Design

Overview

With clients’ brand identities varying markedly in color and graphic treatment, the Within3 platform was intentionally designed to be visually quiet. This simplicity allows each client’s trademark to be easily distinguished, while creating consistency throughout the platform.

In an effort to customize the community experience for each brand, a front-end framework was created to provide maximum flexibility of design and presentation for each community’s individual features.

With this versatility in place, an additional template and theme layer was implemented to streamline each community's speed to market.
