• Launch Collaboration Community
    A Within3 sales tool used to communicate their collabration community experience. This prototype was designed to be used in sales presentations to visually tell a story of how physicians would be invited, register, and collaborate on the Within3 platform.
  • Launch Ad Board Community
    Like the Collaboration Community prototype, the Ad Board Community prototype is a sales tool used to present a story of how physicians are invited, register, and contribute to an Within3 platform advisory board.
  • Launch Metrics Report
    A data visualization to show performance metrics within a given community. Utilizing D3 javascript and SVG library to explore posibile production solutions.
  • Launch Platform Hackathon
    Developed over a hackathon weekend, this project explored a new platform artchitecture that revolved around the user's profile and identity within the community. A story and prototype was created to visualize and communicate new feature development that attempts to guide user experience by hinting at next steps in using the software based interests, connections, and past interaction behavior.
  • Launch Attchments on Comments
    A new feature prototype tool to help communicate requierments to developers.
