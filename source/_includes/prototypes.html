• Launch Collaboration Community
    The Collaboration Community was developed as a sales tool to introduce the community experience on the Within3 platform. This prototype was designed to visually communicate how physicians would be invited, register, and collaborate with their respective communities on the Within3 platform.
  • Launch Ad Board Community
    Like the Collaboration Community, the Ad Board Community is a prototype used by the sales teams to present the process by which a physician would participate and contribute to a Within3 platform advisory board.
  • Launch Metrics Report
    The Metrics Report is a data visualization prototype, displaying the performance metrics within a given community, utilizing D3 javascript and SVG library to explore possible production solutions.
  • Launch Platform Hackathon
    Developed over a hackfest weekend, this project explored new platform architecture which revolved around a user's profile and identity within a community.
