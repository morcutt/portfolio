UX Design Within3 Graphics & Templates / User Interface

Within3 is a online platform that hosts communities in the healthcare and life sciences marketplace. All communities are individualy branded for existing medical associations or other orgaizations that center around a disease state, drug or treatment.

A community uses social media tools that allow member discussions, moderater guided spotlights, member connections, moderated posts, resources sharing (photos, videos, files, etc.), and other forms of content.
