UX Design Within3 Graphics & Templates / User Interface

Within3 is a online platform that hosts communities in the healthcare and life sciences marketplace. All communities are individualy branded for existing medical associations or other orgaizations that center around a disease state, drug or treatment.

A community uses social media tools that allow member discussions, moderater guided spotlights, member connections, moderated posts, resources sharing (photos, videos, files, etc.), and other forms of content.

Within3 Collaboration Community Design

Overview

Because client brands vary in color and graphic treatment, the Within3 platform's pressence was intentionally designed be graphically quiet. This allows their clients brand to shine as well as to create a base consistancey between community experiences.

In an effort to custimize community experience per brand, a front-end framework was created to allow maximum flexibility of design and community feature arrangment.

With this flexibility in place, an additional template and theme layer was implimented to streamline each community's speed to market.

Interactive Email

In an effort to avert login bariers to participation, interactive email was implimented for all member, group, and discussion messaging. Users are now able to respond to colleges and prticipate in discussions from their inbox without having to log into the platform.

Overview

All new features or architecture changes follow a user centerd methodology of researching design patterns and testing solutions to increase participation.

Work is done close with product management, engineering, testing and sales teams from the design process and prototyping through to the feature buildout and release.
